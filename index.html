The Rules

  1. Slack your opponent for the round for their Words With Friends name.
  2. You must complete your game for each round by the deadline for that round. If you do not, whoever is leading at the deadline wins.
  3. Each round is 1 game, but if you and your opponent agree to play more than that, whoever is ahead at that round's deadline moves on (no deliberate clock milking).
  4. After each round, the winner should Slack Scase the winner and the final score.
  5. Once all scores are in, Scase will update the bracket and announce the start and deadline of the next round.

    6. Grand Prize: An office pizza party (probably not tho).
